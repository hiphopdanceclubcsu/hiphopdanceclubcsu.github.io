About the Club!

"X" years ago, Kylie introduced Ernan to the world of hip-hop and street dance. Together, they started the CSU Hip-Hop Dance Club. Their mission, to embody and educate elements of hip-hop and street dance. "Peace, Love, Unity, & Having Fun" is the motto of hip-hop. And "Hip-Hop" stands for "Higher Inner Power Helping Other People". The club has since continued to be a unique space that brings hip-hop to the community.
