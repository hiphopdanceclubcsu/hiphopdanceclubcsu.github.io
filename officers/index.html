Meet our officers!!

  • "I don't love hip-hop for the moves, I love it for the spirit of freedom and having fun that it calls people to. Whether you come once a semester to relax from midterms or pop-in consecutively, this club is for everyone. My hope is that, regardless of your dance experience, you feel welcome here at the CSU Hip-Hop Dance Club!"
    - Matt Bishop,
    President

  • "I joined this club as a way to escape reality for a moment and continue my connection with the dance community at CSU. I’ve always had a love and passion for dance and the Hip Hop Dance Club allows me to continue growing and enjoying this beautiful art form."
    - Melody Cammarata,
    Vice President
    and Financial Officer

  • "I love being apart of the hip hop club because of the supportive environment and ability to learn new styles of hip hop."
    - Allison Howel,
    Organization and
    Public Relations Officer

  • "My hope is that this is a welcoming space for all dancers, regardless of your experience level. This is a place of fun for people who wanna dance. I'm beyond thrilled to represent the team with integrity, commitment to grow, learn, & spread the love of that positive hip hop energy!!"
    - Simone Woodbury,
    Social Media Officer

  • "Hip hop dance culture has all of my favorite things: community, music, creativity, self-expression, collaboration, art, love, and so much more. Dance is an international language. Where words fall short, I find myself more able to communicate in the freedom and beauty of movement."
    - Renee Freeman,
    Vision Chair

  • "This club was the first time I’d danced hip-hop — and now I love it. Our community is full of welcoming and fun people who simply love dancing together. We work to create an accepting environment where everyone feels free to express themselves and have fun through movement. If you like dancing or think you might like dancing, even if you’ve never done it before, this club is for you!
    - Bridget Ediger,
    Senior Ambassador

  • "I want hip hop club to be a place where we can make art accessible to those who need it, teaching and building upon foundations of hip hop styles to create something unique and original"
    - Donovan del Rosario,
    Senior Ambassador

  • "Dancing for me is an exploration of mind and body through expression. Dancing is a meeting with myself, in a given moment. Finding CSU Hip-Hop Dance Club is one of the best things that's happened for me in this period of my life it's a space where I can let loose and be myself."
    - DaVaughn,
    Ambassador
